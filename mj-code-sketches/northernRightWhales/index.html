Northern Atlantic Right Whale Death Count

As I watch whales this summer, I've been saddened by all the challenges that humans pose to Northern Right Whales in particular. Climate change is changing where their food (e.g.plankton) is going, pushing them into Canada, where ships are hitting them. They're getting tangled in fishing gear that can cut into them and exhaust them until they die. Sound pollution by the military is stressing them out. As I've been obsessing over this issue and counting the number of whale deaths in such a small population, I decided to make this infographic.
