Steps to make person card

  • download sublime text, node.js, git from dalton software center
  • create folder on desktop called "my person card"
  • open sublime text. save document as name.html in your new folder
  • Add two photos/drawings into your folder
  • Download person card template and add your images
  • EVERYTHING IN YOUR FODLER to me (molly josephs) as an attachment!
